Lab

The Lab page is where you can connect to and perform actions on your remote system, as well as your students remote systems. Note that the a remote system is also referred to as the remote desktop.

Find the Lab page:

  • Click Lab from the top menu bar.

From the Lab page you can:

  • Connect to your remote system.
  • Connect to your students remote systems.
  • Reboot the remote systems.
  • Transfer files to and from your local system and the remote systems.
  • Reset a remote system to its original state.
  • Deploy a new image to a remote system.
  • Clear the activation information of an access code.
  • Chage access codes.
