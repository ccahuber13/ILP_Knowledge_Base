Editing your profile

Completing profile information and uploading a picture is a great way to re-create a classroom experience.

To edit your profile:

  1. Go to the Lobby page.
  2. Click the pencil icon located in the top-right hand corner of the Your profile panel.
  3. Enter your profile details. To keep specific information private from other participants, click the check-box next to a field.
    4. As an alternative to uploading a profile picture, you can use an email address associated with a Gravatar account. The ILP imports your Gravatar picture as your profile picture.
