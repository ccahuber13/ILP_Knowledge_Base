Changing the portal theme

You can change the default color theme of the Instructor-Led Portal.

To change the default color theme, follow the steps below:

  1. Click your name from the user menu in the top right-hand corner of the portal.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Select your desired theme from the Theme drop-down list, then click Save.
