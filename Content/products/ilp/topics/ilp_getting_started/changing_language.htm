Changing the default language

You can change the default language of the Instructor-Led Portal to one of 14 supported languages.

To change the default language of the ILP, follow the steps below:

  1. Click your name from the user menu in the top right-hand corner of the portal.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Select your desired language from the Language drop-down list, then click Save.
