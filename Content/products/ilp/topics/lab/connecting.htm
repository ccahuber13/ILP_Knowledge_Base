Connecting to a remote lab

Learn how an instructor or student connects to their remote lab, also referred to as the remote lab. By default, users connect to the remote lab using our ReadyTech viewer web client.

To connect to a remote lab, follow the steps below:

  1. Click Lab from the top menu bar.
  2. Click Connect to the lab from the Remote desktop panel.

Desktop actions menu

When connected to a remote lab, a Desktop tab appears on the top menu bar. The Desktop tab contains an Actions menu similar to the one found under the Lab tab. However, Actions performed under the Desktop tab only affect the system you are currently connected to. See Actions menu for more information.

Connecting with different viewers

By default, our ReadyTech Viewer (RTViewer) web client is used to view the remote lab. Alternatively, you may connect with other viewers such as VNC or Remote Desktop.

To learn how to connect with other viewers:

  • Connecting with ReadyTech Viewer desktop client.
  • Connecting with Remote Desktop (RDP).

Disconnecting from the remote lab

To disconnect from a remote lab, follow the steps below:

  • Click Desktop top from the top menu bar, then click disconnect.
