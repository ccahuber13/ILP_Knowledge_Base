Powering on a shutdown remote system

In some cases, a users remote system may be powered off. Use the Power on command in the Actions menu to power on a remote system.

To power on a remote system:

  1. Click Lab from the top menu bar.
  2. Click Actions then click Power on....
  3. Select the check-box next to each user whom you want to power on their system.
  4. Click OK.
