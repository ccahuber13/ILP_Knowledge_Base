Connecting with VNC

As an alternative to connecting with our RTViewer, you can use VNC. Connecting with VNC provides no extra benefits and is limited in its display size and is not compatible with our viewer toolbar.

Try using VNC as an alternative if you are experiencing connection or display related issues with other viewers.

To connect with VNC:

  1. Go to the Lab page.
  2. Choose VNC from the viewer drop-down list.
  3. Click Connect to the lab.
