Blocking user access

From the Actions menu, you can block student access to specific areas of the Instructor-Led Portal such as:

  • The Instructor-Led Portal
  • The Materials tab
  • The Labs tab

To block user access:

  1. Click Lab from the top menu bar.
  2. Click Actions then click Block user access.
  3. Click the check-box of each student where you want to block access to a specific area of the portal.
  4. Click Save.
