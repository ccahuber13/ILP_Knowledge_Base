Resetting remote labs

Use Reset system… to restore a remote lab to its initial, preconfigured state. Be aware that using reset system reinstalls the original software image and wipes out any software and data on the system; any work you have done up to this point is lost.

A cloud-based lab takes about 5 to 10 minutes to reset. A hardware-based lab can take 30 minutes to 2 hours to reset, depending on the complexity of the lab environment. The status panel on the Lab page displays a green upward facing arrow icon indicating when you can connect to the remote lab.

To reset a remote lab, follow the steps below:

  1. Click the Lab tab on the navigation bar, then click Reset system...
  2. Select the check-box next to each student whom you want to reset their systems.
  3. Click Reset from the Reset system prompt.
    Click the Lab tab then select Overview to see the status of the remote labs during a reset.
