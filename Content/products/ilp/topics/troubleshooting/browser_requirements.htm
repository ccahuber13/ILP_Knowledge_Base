Browser requirements

See the following list of supported HTML5 browsers:

  1. Internet Explorer 10 or higher
  2. Microsoft Edge
  3. Firefox 15 or higher
  4. Chrome 16 or higher
  5. Opera 12 or higher
  6. Safari 6 or higher

If HTML5 is not supported, try using a different browser or upgrading your current browser. If the above do not resolve your issue, contact get-support@readytech.com
